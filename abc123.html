Introduction

You are no doubt aware of the revitalisation of te reo Māori, an essential part of Aotearoa New Zealand's heritage. To this end, we aim to design and develop Ako Māori, a web application to learn te reo Māori, assisting beginners. The web app employs a service-oriented architecture, encompassing a UI front-end and a service back-end. The first two assignments asked you to contribute to constructing the service back-end, and this assignment continues your journey in helping to construct the front-end.
This assignment examines the separation of data from the UI, and how such data can be consumed and utilized in the end-user application. A subsequent assignment will delve into potential vulnerabilities that may surface when such an architecture is poorly implemented.
In the first two assignments, you implemented a handful of data sources that provide and update key information content. These data sources facilitate the separation of information content from its presentation.
